Picovania

PicoVania is a demake of the classic NES action platformer Castlevania. The classic game at half the size!

Guide Simon Belmont through Dracula's castle fighting minions and deadly bosses, armed with the Vampire Killer whip and various sub-weapons.

Genre:Action Platformer
Players:1
Controls:Gamepad; Keyboard
Made With:PICO-8; LUA
