Kingdom Over Dungeon

Kingdom Over Dungeon is a hybrid role-playing and management simulation game in which you run a small kingdom, build the capital and order it's citizens to work, while exploring dangerous dungeons, fighting monsters in turn-based combat and gathering resources to take back to your kingdom.

Welcome to a dangerous world, turned upside-down by a magical cataclysm. The prosperous nations of the world were torn asunder and scattered. The ground opened up to reveal countless dungeons filled with terrible monsters, which swarmed out onto the surface attacking people and destroying civilizations.

Small realms managed to resist the initial onslaught, they rebuilt their homes and defences. People came together and struggled against the wild beasts beyond the walls of their new homelands.

With the surface in ruins and resources scarce the only option left to continue rebuilding and expansion was to delve into the myriad maze-like dungeons that filled the land and discover the valuables within and bring them back home.

The game is split into two modes, Kingdom Management and Dungeon Exploration. During the Kingdom Management mode you assign citizens to work in the various buildings of the capital, create a party that will venture into the dungeon, build new buildings, and upgrade existing ones, as well as check the stats, skills, and equipment of citizens.

Once you have decided what you want your citizens to do and grouped a suitable party you can enter the dungeon, where you will find monsters to battle, items and resources to collect and a powerful boss monster: the Dungeon Champion waiting for you.

After you've defeated the Dungeon Champion your surviving party members will return to the castle, resources collected in the dungeon will be added to your kingdom's resources along with any resources generated by the buildings you own and the citizens working in them. DEATH IS PERMANENT, if a citizens dies in battle they are gone forever, even if your entire party dies the game will continue so long as the ruler of your kingdom still lives. If your ruler dies it's game over, you'll have to load a save or start a new game.

There will floors of the dungeon where a citizen has gotten trapped, if you lead them out of the dungeon to safety they will join your kingdom.

Genre:Management/RPG
Players:1
Controls:Gamepad; Keyboard
Made With:RPG Maker; Ruby
