A Little Chaos

A Little Chaos is a remake of the classic strategy game Chaos: Battle of Wizards for ZX Spectrum. 1 to 8 human or CPU-controlled players take turns casting spells and directing their summoned creatures in battle. The last wizard alive is the winner.

Genre:Strategy
Players:1-8
Controls:Gamepad; Keyboard
Made With:PICO-8; LUA
