Deep Space

Deep Space is a text-based adventure game where you explore space and battle enemy spaceships. Your mission is to destroy the Federation Capitol ship, but doing so will require you to upgrade your ship with the powerful weapons you can buy or find along the way.

The game is played using a keyboard, you type answers to the prompts given, usually yes or no, or a number from a list of options.

Turn-based combat, random encounters, branching paths, multiple outcomes to events, ship upgrades and more make Deep Space an exhilarating re-playable adventure.

This game was made in 4 days as part of a Code Nation course by myself, Lucky Ali, Alexander Dean, Imogen Evans, Zachary Finlay, and Ajmal Karim; with help from instructor Dan Monaghan.

Genre:Text Adventure
Players:1
Controls:Keyboard
Made With:Python
